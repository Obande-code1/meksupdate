About Us

Welcome to MEKS Coding & Robotic Club, a creative hub where young innovators explore the exciting world of technology. We provide a fun and engaging environment that empowers kids and teens to learn coding, robotics, game development, and web technologies.

Our mission is to inspire curiosity, spark innovation, and prepare the next generation of problem solvers and tech leaders through hands-on learning, mentorship, and teamwork.
